Lead Instructor at Mathnasium

My commitment to teaching and passion for mathematics led me to become a leadinstructor at Mathnasium in a short period of time. As the lead instructor for a brand new centre I oversaw daily operations, trained new instructors on Mathnasium teaching techniques, conducted diagnostic assessments to identify student's strengths, weaknessess and learning gaps. I would review student progress regularly specially highschool students, and adjust as needed based on school curriculums, progress and baseline understandings. In addition to upholding the Mathnasium standards on all of my instructors I would be the point of contact for most of the highschool students parents. Due to my experience with the Ontario math curriculums from grade 9 to 12, I knew what where the core subjects I wanted my students to focus on and work on during their sessions.

In addition to these responsabilities I would also give the team ideas for activities and games we could play with students to catch their interest in mathematics. Some of these ideas were using the binary number systemat in cards to guess the students number, or using logic engineering to solve true or false riddles in a game called "Knights and Knaves". I also created a short summer camp activity where I taught students how to read and write Python code. I would also love teaching kids about astronomical principles such as what causes the seasons or talking about stars and galaxies in general.

Mathnasium

Mathnasium is a specialized math learning center that provides personalized tutoring and enrichment programs for students in grades K-12. Its mission is to help children develop a deeper understanding of math, improve their confidence, and enhance their skills through a unique teaching methodology known as the Mathnasium Method. This approach emphasizes conceptual understanding and critical thinking over rote memorization.
