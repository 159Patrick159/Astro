Harmonized Life License Qualification Program

I've always been a good presentor and overall great communicator, given my experience in research presentations and tutorships. So took up a position as a Life Insurace Agent for Globe Life - AIL Canada. In order to be able to work in this field I had to take an online course on Life Insurance and pass several proctored exams on this course. Then I had to sign up for the provincial examinations and pass four different proctored exams. I was able to complete all of these requirements in the span of 3 weeks.

Aside from displaying my discipline and effective learning strategies this job drastically helped me become an excellent communicator. In the business of life insurance there is a saying that goes: "First they buy you, second they buy the company, and third they buy the product". And being a life insurance agent is nothing like being a pushy salesman. Primarily because you are selling a "need" not a "want". Your success relies on the quality of the program you offer your clients, if the program is not feasible for a lifetime commitment then you only stand to lose from that. This work helped me connect with people to protect themselves and their future.
